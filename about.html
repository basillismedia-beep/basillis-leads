About Basillis — Founded 2024

Built by someone

Basillis exists because most lead generation for contractors is broken — built around retainers, not results. Here's why we did it differently.

I started Basillis in 2024 after seeing how many home improvement contractors get stuck on the same hamster wheel — busy one month, dead quiet the next, with referrals as the only real source of new work. Most marketing agencies pitch them monthly retainers and promise "exposure," then disappear after the contract is signed, whether the leads actually convert or not.

That setup doesn't make sense for a contractor. You shouldn't pay a marketing company the same amount whether they send you ten great homeowners or zero.

So Basillis works the other way around — you only pay when we actually deliver a qualified lead.

No retainer. No contract. No "trust the process" for three months while nothing happens. We run targeted ads, filter out anyone who isn't a real homeowner with a real project on a real timeline, and the only person who lands in your inbox is someone worth calling.

Before you commit to anything, you can test it for 10 days completely free on our end — you just cover a small ad budget. If the leads aren't worth it, you walk away. If they are, you keep going. Simple as that.

We work with roofing, fencing, remodeling, and other home improvement trades across the US — one contractor per market at a time, so the leads we generate for you aren't being split with your competitor down the street.

What we actually believe

Pay for results, not promises

If a lead isn't qualified, you don't pay for it. That's the whole model — there's no incentive for us to send you junk.

No 3-month trust falls

You see real leads in the first 10 days. If it's not working, you'll know fast — not after a quarter of wasted ad spend.

One contractor per market

We don't sell the same leads to five competing companies in your city. If we're working with you, we're not working against you.
